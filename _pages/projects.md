## Workshop Projects

We will carry out a collective project in the  Humanities Data and Mapping Environments workshop. The theme of the 2026 project is "Doors of Besançon." Participants will also work on their own projects. 

Details will be added here as the workshop progresses.

### Project Guidelines

Participants will work on a hands-on mapping project throughout the workshop. Projects will:

- Create data relevant to the humanities with a spatial dimension
- Practice the skill of in situ mapping
- Employ one or more digital mapping tools introduced during the workshop
- Include critical reflection on methodological choices
- Be accompanied by brief documentation

More details will be shared during the workshop.
