## Workshop Projects

This page will showcase projects created by participants during the Humanities Data and Mapping Environments workshop.

Projects will be added here as the workshop progresses.

### Project Guidelines

Participants will work on a hands-on mapping project throughout the workshop. Projects should:

- Use humanities data in a meaningful way
- Employ one or more digital mapping tools introduced during the workshop
- Include critical reflection on methodological choices
- Be accompanied by brief documentation

More details will be shared during the workshop.
